GHG Emission Scopes

Scope 1: Direct Emissions

Emissions from owned/controlled sources like diesel use, vehicles, and on-site combustion.

Scope 2: Indirect Energy

Emissions from purchased electricity, steam, heating, or cooling used by the organization.

Scope 3: Other Indirect

All other indirect emissions in the value chain, including logistics, business travel, and waste.
